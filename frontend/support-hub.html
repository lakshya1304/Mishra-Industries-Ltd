Order FAQs

How do I track my industrial order?

Once your order is Shipped from our Atlas hub, you will receive a tracking ID via SMS. You can also view status in your Account dashboard.

What payment methods are supported?

We support Credit/Debit Cards, NetBanking, RTGS for bulk orders, and UPI.

Shipping Policy

1. Nationwide Reach: We deliver electrical solutions across India using ISI-certified logistics partners.

2. Handling Time: Most orders are dispatched within 24-48 business hours from our central warehouse.

3. Bulk Logistics: Industrial cables and heavy switchgear may require specialized transport; our team will coordinate directly for such shipments.

Returns & Refunds

1. 7-Day Window: Returns are accepted within 7 days for unused items in original packaging.

2. Non-Returnable: Cut-to-length cables and customized industrial panels are not eligible for returns.

3. Process: Refunds are processed back to the original payment source within 5-7 business days.
